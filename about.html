About Breast Cancer HQ

This application is intended for educational and reference purposes only.

Staging calculations are based on the AJCC 8th Edition Breast Cancer Staging System. While efforts have been made to ensure accuracy, users should independently verify all results and consult official AJCC resources and institutional guidelines before making clinical decisions.

This application is not intended to replace physician judgment, multidisciplinary discussion, or professional medical advice.

This app was created by Stefan Wei, current medical student at the University of Arizona College of Medicine – Phoenix.
